About Roncom

Your trusted networking partner across Uganda

Connecting Uganda, One Network at a Time

Roncom Technology Solutions is a leading networking company serving businesses and individuals across Uganda. With expertise in network configuration, WiFi setup, and troubleshooting, we bring reliable connectivity to urban and rural areas alike.

Founded with a mission to bridge the digital divide in Uganda, Roncom has grown to become a trusted name in networking solutions. From the bustling streets of Kampala to the hills of Rukungiri and the plains of Mbarara, we've set up robust networks that keep people and businesses connected.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and professional networking solutions to every corner of Uganda.

Our Vision

A fully connected Uganda where technology empowers every community and business.
